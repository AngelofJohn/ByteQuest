## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your experience.

## How ByteQuest Uses Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | User authentication | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Security protection | Session |
| `game_state` | Save game progress | Persistent |

These cookies remember your settings and preferences.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the Service.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics ID | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics session | 24 hours |

### Performance Cookies

These cookies help us optimize the Service.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `load_balance` | Server optimization | Session |

## Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

- View cookies stored on your device
- Delete all or specific cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all cookies

**How to manage cookies by browser:**

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings → Privacy → Cookies

### Our Cookie Preferences

You can manage non-essential cookies through our cookie consent banner or by contacting us.

### Opt-Out Links

- Google Analytics: [Google Analytics Opt-Out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
